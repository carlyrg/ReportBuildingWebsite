Table Diagrams

Activity Facts
Contains details on all activities including cancelled, completed, pending and rescheduled activities, deposits, tours, move-ins, ratios, etc. Relationships allow for additional details on Assigned Users, Referring Contacts, Campaigns, Individuals and Market Source.
Campaign Facts
Contains details on campaigns, including attendance, budget, invites, RSVPs, etc. Relationship allow for additional details on locations/communities, individuals, contacts and market source.
Goal Community Facts
Contains details on the community goals, variance and KPIs, which are reported from the admin section. Relationships allow for additional details about goals and locations/communities
Relation Facts
Contains details on relationships between contacts and individuals (pre-lead, prospect, residents). Relationships allow for further details on individuals and contacts.
Transaction Facts
Contains details on transactions such as amount, status, date and number of transactions. Relationships allow for further details on units, locations/communities and individuals.
Housing Contract Facts
Contains details on housing contracts including availability, occupancy, move-ins/outs, transfers, etc. Relationships allow for additional details on Units and Locations/communities
Waitlist Facts
Contains details on waitlist including floorplan rank, waitlist individuals, waitlist rank, preference, etc, as reported in the waitlist module. Relationships allow for further details on locations/communities and individuals.
